Frequently Asked Questions

Need Answers? Find them here.

What is Lahari?

+
The annual fest and stall exhibition of Mankar College, Lahari (লহরী), is a vibrant celebration of culture, creativity, and community. This highly anticipated event brings together students, faculty, and visitors to showcase talent, innovation, and entrepreneurial spirit through a variety of cultural programs, stalls, and exhibitions. It serves as a platform to experience the college's rich traditions while fostering a sense of togetherness and festive enthusiasm.

Who can attend Lahari?

+
Lahari warmly welcomes anyone with a passion for technology, innovation, development, and community-building to immerse themselves in an exciting blend of creativity, collaboration, and cultural celebration, making it an ideal platform to connect with like-minded individuals, exchange ideas, and explore opportunities for growth and learning in a vibrant and dynamic environment.

What makes Lahari unique?

+
Lahari's stall exhibitions stand out as a hub of innovation and interaction, offering hands-on learning experiences and the chance to engage directly with technology experts. Whether you’re a tech enthusiast, a budding developer, or simply curious, Lahari creates a unique platform for networking, skill-building, and exploring cutting-edge ideas in an energetic and collaborative atmosphere.

How can I stay informed?

+
Stay up-to-date with all the latest news and updates about Lahari by following the official social media channels of Mankar College's Computer Science Department. For exclusive insights, event schedules, and behind-the-scenes content, subscribe to their newsletter and never miss an opportunity to be part of the excitement!
